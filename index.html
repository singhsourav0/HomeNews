Let's explore this newsroom in detail

In today's fast-paced world, keeping up with the news can be overwhelming. That’s why we built News Summarizer, an AI-powered platform that delivers concise, real-time news summaries—just like Inshorts.
Unlike social media platforms like Instagram, where news is often mixed with distractions, endless scrolling, and unreliable sources, our platform ensures you get only the essential news updates—quickly and efficiently. With social media, you may spend hours consuming unnecessary content, losing valuable time, and encountering misinformation.

Our platform brings you the latest news in the most efficient way possible. Here’s how it works:

Automated News Collection – Our system fetches news directly from BBC News every few minutes.
AI-Powered Summarization – Using advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP), we extract key insights and present short, easy-to-read summaries.
Real-Time Updates – Thanks to GitHub Actions automation, our news feed is always fresh—no outdated articles!
User-Friendly Interface – Enjoy a smooth, visually appealing experience with our scrollable card-based UI.

For our mobile app, we sync news data with Firebase Realtime Database, ensuring seamless and instant updates. For the website, we leverage GitHub Pages for hosting, while GitHub Actions automates fetching, processing, and deploying the latest news effortlessly.

Stay informed effortlessly with quick and concise news updates!
